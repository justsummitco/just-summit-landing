# What On-Device AI Means for Meeting Notes

On-device AI means more of the processing happens on hardware you control rather than on a remote server. For meeting notes, that can change the privacy, speed, cost, and reliability of the whole product.

It does not automatically make a tool private or better. It does create a different design starting point.

## Why It Matters for Audio

Audio is not like a weather query or a generic search. It can contain names, plans, health details, commercial information, and personal context. Sending every recording to the cloud by default is convenient, but it is not always the best trust model.

On-device AI can help by reducing how much raw audio needs to leave the device.

## The Constraints Are Real

Local processing has limits. Hardware must manage:

- Battery life.
- Heat.
- Memory.
- Model size.
- Latency.
- Storage.
- Recovery if power is lost.

That is why on-device AI is not just a software decision. It affects the whole product architecture.

## What It Could Enable

For meeting notes and audio recall, on-device-first design can support:

- Faster capture decisions.
- Better offline behaviour.
- More user control over storage.
- Selective sync instead of automatic upload.
- A clearer privacy promise.

The first-batch product is still being built, so the team will keep sharing clearer details as the hardware, companion app, and processing choices progress.

## Where Just Summit Fits

Just Summit Headphones are being built around an on-device-first direction for wearable audio capture and summarisation. The aim is to help people keep useful spoken information without treating cloud upload as the default answer to every problem.

The product is still pre-production. The current presale supports prototype and first-batch work, with estimated Q4 2026 delivery for the first batch.

## What Buyers Should Ask

Before trusting any AI meeting notes product, ask:

- What happens on device?
- What happens in the cloud?
- What is stored?
- What is encrypted?
- What can I delete?
- What happens if I stop using the product?

On-device AI is not magic. But when it is designed carefully, it can make AI meeting notes feel less like surveillance and more like personal recall.
